LinkedIn is exhausting.

It has become the epitome of performant writing to just get clicks or views.

Oftentimes flaunting headlines/hooks like:

- "We are reinventing an industry..."
- "I did XYZ... What I found will shock you..."
- "Don't get left behind..."

I feel like I don't even remember what the LinkedIn feed was before it became filled with "linkedin influencers". I would argue it's worse than short time video at times.

I don't even know what value I am supposed to get out of the platform anymore.

It used to be a feed of just friends and coworkers and their professional career updates. I guess there is a bit of lack of depth there, but it feels like it has taken a complete 180 now.

I seldom see anything on my LinkedIn feed that is related to anybody I know, or even have any degree of connection with.

Furthermore, I rarely EVER see a post related to an actual job update (new job, promotion, etc).
